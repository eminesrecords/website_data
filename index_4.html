Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 5, 2024

5.1 Access and Correction :

You have the right to access and correct any personal information you have provided to us.

5.2 Opt-Out :

You may opt out of receiving promotional emails by following the unsubscribe link in those communications or by contacting us directly.

8. Contact Us :

If you have any questions, comments, or concerns about this Privacy Policy or your privacy rights, please contact us at : eminesrecordsofficial@gmail.com
